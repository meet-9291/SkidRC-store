The Story of Skid RC

Skid RC wasn't born in a boardroom; it was born in a garage, covered in grease and fueled by a lifelong obsession with all things remote-controlled. We're a small team of engineers and hobbyists who believe that RC cars should be more than just toys—they should be an adrenaline rush in a small package.

We spent countless hours designing, testing, and breaking things just to build them back stronger. Our mission is simple: to create the most durable, high-performance, and ridiculously fun micro RC cars on the market. Every model we sell is one we'd personally love to race.
